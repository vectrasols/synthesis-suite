# Code of Conduct

synthesis-suite should be a welcoming project for contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreement.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Help newcomers understand project patterns and contribution expectations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Dismissing reports of security, safety, or conduct issues in bad faith.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer. Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or block contributors when needed to keep the project healthy.
